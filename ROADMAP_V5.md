## 🚀 Future Frontier: v5.0 "The Deal Architect"
The goal of v5.0 is to absorb the core functionality of Bigin CRM while maintaining the specialized design/execution rigor of Meaven.

### **1. The Sales Pipeline (The "Bigin" Killer)**
*   **Visual Deal Board**: A clean, high-density kanban for incoming projects.
*   **Direct Pricing Engine Integration**: Move from "Lead" to "Quote" in one click.
*   **Email Intelligence**: 
    *   **IMAP/Nodemailer Integration**: Send emails directly via Zoho mail from the dashboard.
    *   **Open Tracking (Pixel Tracking)**: Know exactly when a client opens your proposal.
*   **The Daily Action Center**: A simplified task-list/activity-log for every lead to ensure zero missed follow-ups.

### **2. Automated Proposal Design**
*   **Magazine-Grade PDFs**: Convert Pricing Engine BOMs into stunning, client-ready PDF proposals automatically.
*   **Digital Acceptance**: One-click "Approve" button for clients that triggers the project start in the Hub.

### **3. Vendor Performance Ledger**
*   **Profit Leakage Analytics**: Track which vendors consistently trigger "Hand-Twist" negotiations.
*   **Lead-Time Reliability**: Visualizing who delivers fastest vs. who has the most site-readiness delays.

### **4. Mobile Executive Terminal**
*   **PWA Enhancements**: Push notifications for SOS site alerts directly to the CEO's phone.
