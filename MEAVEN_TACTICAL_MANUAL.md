# Meaven Intelligence v5.5: Strategic Operator's Manual

## 🌐 Overview
**Meaven Intelligence (MI)** is a high-fidelity, data-driven execution platform designed for lean project management and vendor orchestration. It transitions complex architectural and design projects into manageable tactical loops, providing real-time visibility from site audits to global financial EBITDA.

---

## 🔐 1. Security & Authentication
The platform utilizes a multi-layered defense architecture to ensure data integrity and confidentiality.

*   **Primary Access**: Secure email/password login.
*   **Security PIN (Tactical Gate)**: Switching from "Client Mode" to "Internal Tactical Mode" requires a secondary 4-digit PIN.
*   **Master Key (Founder Override)**: A universal master key (`210805`) allows bypass for executive emergency access.
*   **Automated Audit Trail**: Every login is automatically logged in the history of every active project as "Tactical Access Authorized," identifying the operator and timestamp.

---

## 📊 2. Strategic Executive Hub (CEO View)
*The macro-intelligence dashboard designed for Founder-level oversight.*

*   **Portfolio Macro-Financials**: Real-time aggregation of Total Portfolio Value, Realized Revenue (Cash-in-hand), and Vendor Liabilities.
*   **Vendor Concentration Risk**: A proprietary tracking system that flags if any single vendor handles >40% of project volume to prevent operational bottlenecks.
*   **Pipeline Velocity**: A high-level visualization of project distribution across all execution stages.
*   **Global Health Index**: The average "Readiness Score" across the entire enterprise.

---

## 📁 3. Project Central (Execution Hub)
*The primary workspace for project-level tactical management.*

*   **Financial Intelligence (P&L)**: Dual-track ledger capturing:
    *   **Client Revenue**: Every payment received from clients with mandatory date, amount, and transaction IDs.
    *   **Vendor Payouts**: Precise tracking of money leaving the ecosystem to partners.
*   **Audit History**: A non-editable timeline capturing every project event, update, and login access.
*   **Milestone Tracking**: Real-time management of site measurements, readiness dates, and completion targets.

---

## 🤝 4. Vendor Bench (Partner Scoring)
*The engine for lean outsourcing management.*

*   **MI Score (Intelligence Rating)**: Vendors are scored based on their historical performance and data accuracy.
*   **Capacity Management**: Visibility into how many active projects a vendor is currently handling.
*   **Universal Onboarding**: Ability to register new vendors directly from a specific project sheet and assign them instantly.

---

## 📏 5. Live Audit & Readiness Hub
*Bridging the gap between the field and the dashboard.*

*   **Site Readiness Scoring**: A checklist-driven scoring system (0-100%) that determines if a site is ready for execution.
*   **Geospatial Verification**: Locking project coordinates to ensure audits are happening on the actual site.
*   **Digital Documentation**: Uploading site photos and technical audit data directly to the cloud.

---

## ☁️ 6. Technical Foundation: Supabase Cloud
*   **Global Sync**: Meaven Intelligence is powered by **Supabase**. All data is synchronized in real-time across all devices.
*   **Zero-Loss Migration**: The platform features an intelligent bridge that migrates local browser data to the cloud automatically upon first login.
*   **Offline Resilience**: Utilizes a "Local-First" approach where changes are saved to the browser instantly and synced to the cloud whenever a connection is active.

---

## 🔄 7. Navigation & Workflow
*   **Tactical Navigation**: Use the sidebar to switch between core loops (Command Center, Projects, Vendors, Audit).
*   **Global Back Button**: Use the **← BACK** button in the header to reverse through your navigation history without losing state.
*   **Project Initialization**: Launch new "Project Loops" or "Client Portfolios" using the quick-access buttons in the sidebar.

---

**Operator Note**: Meaven Intelligence is designed to scale your revenue without scaling your team. Use the data to manage the vendors, and the platform to manage the data.
